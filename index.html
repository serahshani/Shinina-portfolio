Human Rights

I advocate passionately for human rights, championing the fundamental freedoms that every individual deserves. I work tirelessly to uphold the right to life, ensuring safety and security for all. I stand against torture and cruelty,
advocating for humane treatment and dignity in every situation. I fight for liberty and justice, promoting freedom from oppression and arbitrary detention.
Through my activism, I strive for freedom of expression, empowering voices to be heard without fear.

Program management

In addition to my advocacy for human rights, I excel in program management, where I coordinate and oversee initiatives aimed at creating meaningful impact.
Through strategic planning and meticulous execution, I ensure that projects are efficiently implemented to achieve their objectives. I am adept at managing resources, timelines, and stakeholders, fostering collaboration and driving progress toward shared goals.
